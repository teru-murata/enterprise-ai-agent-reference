# Incident Response Policy

This synthetic policy describes how the fictional company handles internal incidents.

## Severity Levels

- Severity 1: Customer-facing outage, data exposure risk, or critical security event.
- Severity 2: Major degradation affecting multiple customers or core internal workflows.
- Severity 3: Limited degradation with a known workaround.

## Response Steps

1. Open an incident record.
2. Assign an incident commander.
3. Capture timeline, decisions, customer impact, and remediation steps.
4. Notify support leadership for Severity 1 and Severity 2 incidents.
5. Complete a post-incident review within five business days.

All incident actions must be auditable.
