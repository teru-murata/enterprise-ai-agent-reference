# Customer Support FAQ

This synthetic FAQ helps support agents respond to common incident-related questions.

## Customer Updates

Support may draft customer-facing updates during an incident, but the incident commander must review them before sending.

## Ticket Drafts

Support agents may prepare draft tickets that summarize symptoms, severity, affected systems, and observed timestamps. Drafts do not create real tickets until approved.

## Escalation

Severity 1 and Severity 2 issues should be escalated to support leadership and the incident commander.
